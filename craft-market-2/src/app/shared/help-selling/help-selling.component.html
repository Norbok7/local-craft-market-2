Welcome to Our Craft Store!

Create an Artisan Account:

  1. Click here to access the artisan login page.
  2. Select "Create Account" to sign up as an artisan.
  3. Fill out the required information to set up your artisan account.
  4. Once your account is created, you'll be redirected to your artisan profile.

List Your Products:

  1. Navigate to your artisan profile.
  2. Click on the "Add Product" button to add new products.
  3. Fill out the product details form with information about your craft items.
  4. Submit the form to list your products for sale.

View Product Sales:

  1. After listing your products, you can view your product sales on your artisan profile page.
  2. Navigate to your artisan profile to see the list of products you've sold.
  3. Stay updated on your sales and craft items inventory.
