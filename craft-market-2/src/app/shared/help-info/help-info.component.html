Welcome to Our Craft Store!

Create an Account:

  1. Click here to log in.
  2. Select "Create Account".
  3. Follow the prompts to set up your account.
  4. Once your account is created, you'll be automatically logged in.

Explore Our Products:

  1. Navigate to the Products page.
  2. Use the filters to find products by name or price.
  3. Click on a product to view more details.
  4. Add desired items to your cart or continue browsing.

View Your Cart:

  1. Click on the shopping cart icon in the top right corner.
  2. Review the items in your cart.
  3. Proceed to checkout if you're ready to make a purchase.

Checkout:

  1. Click here to view your cart and proceed with your order.
  2. If you're not already logged in, you'll be prompted to log in or create an account.
  3. Follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

View Your Orders:

  1. After completing a purchase, you can view your current order status.
  2. Navigate to the Orders page to see your past orders.
  3. Enjoy your craft items!
